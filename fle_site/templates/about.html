About

The Foundation for Learning Equality (FLE), currently in the process of incorporation as a California-based nonprofit organization, is dedicated to creating tools for the sharing and creation of open-licensed educational content for use by anyone around the world, with special emphasis on reaching the 65% of the world that does not have Internet access.
